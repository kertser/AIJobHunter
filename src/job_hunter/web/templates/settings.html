🔗 LinkedIn Session

LinkedIn requires an authenticated session for job discovery and applications. The easiest way is to copy the li_at cookie from your browser.

Checking…

🍪 Paste your li_at cookie

How to get it:
  1. Log in to linkedin.com in your browser
  2. Open DevTools (F12 or Ctrl+Shift+I)
  3. Go to Application → Cookies → https://www.linkedin.com
  4. Find the li_at cookie and copy its Value
📁 Alternative: Upload full cookies JSON file

Use a browser extension like Cookie-Editor to export all LinkedIn cookies as JSON and upload the file here.

🖥️ Last resort: Remote Login (log in from the server — may trigger CAPTCHA)

Log in to LinkedIn using a headless browser on the server. Use this when you can't export cookies from a local browser (e.g. Docker deployment).

⚠️ What to expect

LinkedIn may ask for a verification code sent to your email or phone. The system will automatically click "Send code" if possible. Watch the log below for status.

If you don't receive a code, LinkedIn may be blocking automated login from this IP. In that case, use the cookie upload method above instead.

If LinkedIn shows a CAPTCHA or "approve from app" prompt, programmatic login won't work — use cookie upload.
